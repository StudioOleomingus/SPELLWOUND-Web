SPELLWOUND

A CROSSWORD OF SLIDING LETTER-TRAINS

LEVELS

COMPLETE THE CROSSWORD BY SLIDING LETTER-TRAINS INTO POSITION

CLICK AND DRAG A HEAD OR A TAIL INTO AN EMPTY BLOCK TO MOVE A TRAIN. A HEAD / TAIL IS A BLOCK AT THE START OR AT END OF A TRAIN - THEY EACH HAVE AN EMPTY TRIANGULAR HOLE OR A NOTCH IN THEM.

YOU CAN ONLY PULL A TRAIN INTO AN EMPTY SPACE NOT PUSH IT.

TRAINS TRAVEL ONLY ON THE DOTTED TRACK - THE CROSSWORD BLOCKS AND THE GRAY BLOCKS WHERE TRAINS BEGIN. THE REST OF THE GRID IS SCENERY.

THE TRAINS SLOT INTO SPECIFIC POSITIONS ON THE GRID TO FORM WORDS - AND THE COLOURED TRIANGLES ON THE EMPTY GRID ARE MARKS THAT SHOW WHERE EACH HEAD OR TAIL OF A TRAIN IS SUPPOSED TO SLOT. WHEN A HEAD OR TAIL RESTS ON ITS MARK, ITS NOTCH FILLS IN.

THE ADD & REMOVE ON THE BOTTOM LEFT WILL ADD OR REMOVE THESE COLOURED TRIANGLES. ADDING TRIANGLES TO THE GRID WILL MAKE THE PUZZLE EASIER (IF YOU ARE STUCK) AND REMOVING THEM WILL MAKE IT HARDER (IF YOU LIKE A CHALLENGE).

LIKE ANY TYPICAL CROSSWORD, YOU CAN CLICK ON AN EMPTY GRID BLOCK TO GET A CROSSWORD CLUE.
